# Day1 Health Plans Analysis - Single Member Pricing (1 Adult, No Children)

## Overview
Day1 Health offers 5 main insurance plans. This analysis focuses on the Single Member option only (1 adult, no children).

---

## 1. DAY-TO-DAY PLAN

### Pricing
**R385/month** (Single member, no children)

### Key Benefits
- Unlimited Doctor Visits (R300 co-payment after 5th visit per member per annum)
- Acute/Chronic Medication
- Pathology (basic diagnostic blood tests)
- Specialist Benefit (up to R1,000 per family per annum)
- Basic Dentistry (2 visits per member per annum)
- Optometry (one eye test + glasses every 24 months)
- Out-of-Area Visits (3 per family per annum)
- Radiology (basic x-rays)
- Family Funeral Benefit (R10,000 principal/spouse/child >14yrs; R5,000 child >6yrs; R2,500 child >0yrs; R1,250 stillborn >28wks)

### Waiting Periods
- 1 month: Doctor visits, pathology, acute medication, out-of-area visits, radiology
- 3 months: Specialist benefit, dentistry, chronic medication (unknown conditions), funeral benefit
- 12 months: Chronic medication (pre-existing conditions), optometry

---

## 2. HOSPITAL PLAN

### Pricing
**R390/month** (Single member, no children)

### Key Benefits
- **1st Day in Hospital**: Up to R10,000
- **2nd Day in Hospital**: Up to R10,000 (payable in R2,500 units for every 6 hours)
- **3rd Day in Hospital**: Up to R10,000 (payable in R2,500 units for every 6 hours)
- **Subsequent Days**: R1,500/day (up to 21 days max)
- **Maximum Benefit**: Up to R57,000 for 21-day period
- **Accident/Trauma Benefit**: Up to R150,000 per single member; R300,000 per family incident
- **24-Hour Emergency Services**: Ambulance & pre-authorisation (0861 144 144)
- **Maternity Benefit**: Up to R20,000 for hospital birth
- **Family Funeral Benefit**: Principal R20,000; Spouse/Child >14yrs R10,000; Child >6yrs R5,000; Child >0yrs R2,500; Stillborn >28wks R1,250

### Waiting Periods
- Immediate: Accident/trauma, emergency services
- 3 months: Hospital illness, funeral benefit
- 12 months: Pre-existing conditions exclusion

---

## 3. COMPREHENSIVE PLAN

### Pricing
**R665/month** (Single member, no children - Value Plus tier)

### Plan Tiers
- **Value Plus**: R665/month
- **Platinum**: R895/month
- **Executive**: R985/month

### Key Benefits (Base)
- Unlimited Doctor Visits (R300 co-payment after 5th visit)
- Acute/Chronic Medication
- Pathology (basic diagnostic blood tests)
- Specialist Benefit (up to R1,000 per family per annum)
- Basic Dentistry (2 visits per member per annum)
- Optometry (one eye test + glasses every 24 months)
- Out-of-Area Visits (3 per family per annum)
- Radiology (basic x-rays)
- **In-Hospital Illness Benefit**: Up to R10,000 per day (first 3 days); R1,500/day thereafter (max 21 days)
- **Accident/Trauma Benefit**: Up to R150,000 (single); R300,000 (family)
- **Critical Illness Benefit**: Up to R250,000 (limited to R50,000 without medical exam)
- **Accidental Permanent Disability**: R250,000 (principal only)
- **24-Hour Emergency Services**: Ambulance & pre-authorisation
- **Maternity Benefit**: Up to R20,000 for hospital birth
- **Family Funeral Benefit**: Principal R20,000; Spouse/Child >14yrs R10,000; Child >6yrs R5,000; Child >0yrs R2,500; Stillborn >28wks R1,250

### Enhanced Benefits (Platinum/Executive)
- **In-Hospital Illness Benefit**: Up to R10,000 per day (first 3 days); R2,000/day thereafter (max 21 days)
- **Illness Top-up**: Up to R25,000 per person per year (max 2 events per family per annum)
- **Accident/Trauma Benefit**: Up to R250,000 (single); R500,000 (family)
- **Accidental Permanent Disability**: R250,000 (principal only)

### Waiting Periods
- 1 month: Doctor visits, pathology, acute medication, out-of-area visits, radiology
- 3 months: Specialist benefit, dentistry, chronic medication (unknown), hospital illness, funeral benefit, critical illness
- 12 months: Chronic medication (pre-existing), optometry, maternity
